ABOUT ME

Why I Do What I Do

Data has always been more than numbers to me. Every dataset tells a story about people, businesses, and the decisions they make. What excites me most is uncovering those stories and transforming them into insights that help organizations make smarter decisions.

Over the past three years, I've worked across healthcare, finance, telecommunications, customer experience, HR, market research, and FMCG. Whether I'm analyzing patient outcomes, loan defaults, employee attrition, customer support performance, sales trends, or business operations, my goal remains the same— to turn complex data into actionable insights that create measurable business value.
