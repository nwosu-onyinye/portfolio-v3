ABOUT ME

Why I Do What I Do

Data has always been more than numbers to me. Every dataset tells a story about people, businesses, and the decisions they make. What excites me most is uncovering those stories and turning them into insights that help organizations make smarter decisions.

Over the past three years, I've worked across healthcare, finance, telecommunications, customer experience, HR, market research, and FMCG, using data to solve business problems through interactive dashboards, reporting, and analytics.

Whether I'm analyzing loan defaults, employee attrition, patient outcomes, customer support performance, or sales trends, my goal remains the same—to transform complex data into actionable insights that create measurable business value.
